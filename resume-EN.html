Research Profile

Researcher developing compilation, execution, and statistical analysis methods to make large-scale scientific computing faster, more reproducible, and numerically reliable. My research connects high-performance computing (HPC), software systems, and floating-point computation analysis: LLVM instrumentation, heterogeneous CPU/GPU execution, vectorized stochastic arithmetic, mixed and adaptive precision, parallel scientific workflows, and reliability testing for AI and biomedical pipelines. My research program aims to develop an open, end-to-end software stack capable of observing, quantifying, and controlling numerical uncertainty on heterogeneous HPC systems while improving performance, memory footprint, and energy efficiency.
